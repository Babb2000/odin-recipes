Taco

Description

Who doesn't love a Taco? The Taco is a traditional Mexican dish usually consisting
of corn or wheat tortilla, topped with a filling of your choice. Famous franchises like Chipotle offer
fillings such as chicken, steak carnitas, and fajita veggies to name a few. While also offering salad
bowls as an alternative to the standard tortilla or taco shell.
